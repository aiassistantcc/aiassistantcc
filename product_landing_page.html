Private AI Assistant

App Features

Private AI Assistant offers a wide range of features designed to give you full control over AI model execution while maintaining your privacy. Here are some key features:

  • Offline AI Execution: Run AI models directly on your device without needing an internet connection, ensuring complete privacy.
  • Custom Model Support: Import your own custom gguf models or download open-source models from Hugging Face to suit your specific needs.
  • Wide Model Compatibility: Supports a broad array of models such as LLaMA, Mistral, Falcon, BERT, Baichuan, Starcoder, and many more.
  • Ollama Server Integration: Connect to Ollama-compatible servers via LAN or trusted networks for centralized deployment of custom models.
  • Privacy-First Design: No cloud dependencies, giving you the freedom to work with sensitive data securely and privately.
